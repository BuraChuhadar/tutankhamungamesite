## Copper Mine

The Copper Mine is built over copper deposits and extracts a dependable industrial metal for workshops and trade. It requires labor, road access, and nearby storage support.

![Copper Mine](/assets/blog/images/2026-04-03-mine-and-military-production-chain-03-bronze-foundry.png)
<p class="text-sm text-center text-gray-500 mt-2">Copper extraction at the start of the production chain.</p>

---

## Charcoal Pit

The Charcoal Pit is built near charcoal deposits and produces fuel for the Bronze Foundry. It requires labor, road access, and nearby storage support.

![Charcoal Pit](/assets/blog/images/2026-04-03-mine-and-military-production-chain-04-royal-arsenal.png)
<p class="text-sm text-center text-gray-500 mt-2">Fuel production that keeps bronze working supplied.</p>

---

## Bronze Foundry

The Bronze Foundry combines copper ore and charcoal to produce bronze, which is then used in the Royal Arsenal for weapon production. It requires labor, road access, and nearby storage support.

![Bronze Foundry](/assets/blog/images/2026-04-03-mine-and-military-production-chain-05-recruitment.png)
<p class="text-sm text-center text-gray-500 mt-2">Bronze production links raw extraction to military manufacturing.</p>

---

## Royal Arsenal

The Royal Arsenal forges bronze into weapons and armaments for the city's armies. It requires labor, road access, and nearby storage support.

![Royal Arsenal](/assets/blog/images/2026-04-03-mine-and-military-production-chain-06-archer-military.png)
<p class="text-sm text-center text-gray-500 mt-2">Finished military equipment is produced at the Royal Arsenal.</p>

---

## Recruitment

Recruitment creates and trains new soldiers for your city's armies. It requires labor and road access.

![Recruitment building](/assets/blog/images/2026-04-03-mine-and-military-production-chain-07-spearman-military.png)
<p class="text-sm text-center text-gray-500 mt-2">The Recruitment building is where trained military forces enter the city.</p>
